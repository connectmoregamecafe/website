Our Story

Analog Hearts in a Digital World

Hi, we’re Jake and Kate Habegger. Like many people today, we found ourselves spending more time looking at notifications than at each other. We realized that some of our best memories weren't made in front of a screen, but across a tabletop.

Connect More started as a simple idea: what if we could bring that feeling to our community in Franklin? We wanted to create a space where the wifi password doesn't matter, but the next move does.
