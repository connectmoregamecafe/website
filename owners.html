The Faces Behind the Games

Jake Habegger

Game Connector

By day, I’m a middle school computer science teacher, helping students navigate the world of coding and logic. But I’ve always believed that while technology is a tool, real connection is a human experience.

My love for games started with the complexity of strategy—seeing how different mechanics can create entirely different outcomes. I started 3D printing custom game components as a hobby, and it wasn't long before I realized I wanted to share the broader "tabletop" experience with others.

Kate Habegger

The Community Builder

I’ve always been the person who wants to host. For me, board games are the perfect "social icebreaker"—they give people a reason to sit down, stay a while, and truly engage with whoever is sitting across from them.

I’m the one focusing on the cafe atmosphere and the "Gateway Games"—the titles that are so fun and intuitive they can turn anyone into a board gamer. Seeing a group of strangers go from quiet to cheering during a game of Codenames is exactly why we started this.

Addy Habegger

Hostess with the Mostess

Addy is the heart of our hospitality. As our lead hostess, she is the first smiling face you’ll see when you walk through our doors. She believes that a great gaming experience starts the moment you arrive, and she takes pride in making every guest feel like a regular.

With a natural talent for organization and a warm, welcoming spirit, Addy ensures that every group finds the perfect spot to settle in. Whether she’s managing the table waitlist or handing out our library passes, she is dedicated to making sure your visit to Connect More is seamless and inviting from start to finish.

Emma Kate Habegger

Game Connector with the Scepter

Emma Kate has been playing games for as long as she can remember. She has a keen sense for strategy and is here to win! When you need someone to get you started on a new game, Emma Kate is your go-to gal!

Her specialty is asking the right questions to help you find the perfect game. If you're not sure where to start, Emma Kate is usually nearby with a recommendation that's guaranteed to get the table talking.
