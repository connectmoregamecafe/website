The Faces Behind the Games

Jake Habegger

Operations Director, Lead Game Connector

With over fifteen years as a dedicated educator, Jake brings a profound understanding of engaging diverse audiences and fostering interactive learning environments. His passion for games and creating enjoyable atmospheres stems from a lifetime of positive family game experiences.

Jake's expertise is instrumental in curating our game library and developing engaging gameplay experiences. Additionally, his five years of experience as a barista at McGavocks Coffee Bar & Provisions provides the operational insight needed to manage the moving parts of our pop-up cafe.

Kate Habegger

Experience Director

A social worker with over ten years of experience, Kate is deeply committed to fostering connection and inclusivity. Her professional background has honed her skills in relationship building, empathetic communication, and operational management.

Kate leads our efforts in customer relations, community partnerships, and designing our functional, welcoming spaces. She ensures that every Connect More event is a safe, accepting, and inclusive environment for everyone in our community.
