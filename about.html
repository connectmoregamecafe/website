Our Mission & Vision

A Vibrant Community Hub

"Forging meaningful connections through the joy of tabletop gaming and shared experiences."

In an increasingly disconnected world, Connect More Game Cafe offers a vital solution. We are more than just another activity; we are a positive, uplifting environment designed for fellowship and creating lasting memories. We aim to be Franklin’s premier destination for quality entertainment, providing a much-needed escape from the digital noise.

The Foundation

Founded by Jake and Kate Habegger, Connect More is built on a robust foundation of community building and professional management. By combining their years of experience in education, social work, and operations, they have designed an experience that goes beyond the game board.

Community

Fostering fellowship through social interaction and shared joy.

Experience

Providing quality, non-digital entertainment for all ages and skill levels.
