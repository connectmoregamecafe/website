More Than Just Games

In a world that is increasingly digital, we found ourselves missing the simple joy of sitting across a table from friends and family. Connect More Cafe was born in Franklin, TN, out of a desire to create a "third space"—a place that isn't work and isn't home, where the only thing you need to focus on is the person in front of you.

We believe that board games are more than just cardboard and plastic; they are tools for connection, laughter, and building community. Our pop-up events bring people together over strategy, luck, and shared experiences. Whether you are a lifelong gamer or haven't picked up a dice in years, there is a seat at our table for you.

Our Mission

"To provide a curated analog experience that fosters real-world relationships and strengthens the social fabric of our local community."
