What exactly is a Board Game Cafe?

A board game cafe is a social space designed for friends, families, and even strangers to gather over a shared activity. Unlike a traditional cafe where you might sit with a laptop, we encourage you to put the screens away. You pay a small cover fee for access to our curated library of 44+ games, and we provide the snacks, drinks, and expertise to make sure you have a great time.

Do you teach people how to play the games?

Absolutely! That is the "Connect More" difference. Our **Game Connectors** (like Jake and Emma Kate) are experts at our library. If you find a game you like but don't want to spend 30 minutes reading a manual, just ask! We love helping you skip the rules and start playing immediately.

Is this a permanent location or a pop-up?

We are currently operating as a high-end **pop-up experience** in Franklin, TN. We partner with local venues to host dedicated gaming nights. This allows us to bring the community together in different spaces while we scout for our perfect permanent home!

Is Connect More kid-friendly?

Very much so! We have a dedicated "Kids" section in our library with games specifically chosen for younger players. We believe that learning to win and lose gracefully is a great life skill, and board games are the perfect way to teach it.

Can I bring my own games?

You are more than welcome to bring your favorites! However, we think you'll find something new to love in our curated collection. Please note that the "Game Cover" fee still applies to help us keep our space open and our staff ready to assist.

What kind of food and drinks do you serve?

We focus on "finger-friendly" snacks that won't mess up the game boards. Think local coffees, specialty sodas, and curated treats that keep your energy up for a long strategy session.
