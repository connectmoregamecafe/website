Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Board Game Cafe?
Think of it as a community living room! A board game cafe is a social space where you can gather with friends or family to enjoy a library of hundreds of tabletop games while grabbing a drink or a snack. It’s a place designed to help people disconnect from screens and Connect More with each other.
How does the "Library Fee" work?
For a small flat fee, you get unlimited access to our collection of over 500 board games for the entire day. Members play for free!
Can I bring my own games?
Absolutely! You are welcome to bring your own games to play at our tables. Standard table rates still apply to cover the space.
Do you teach people how to play?
Yes! Our "Game Gurus" are always on hand to help you pick a game, set it up, and explain the rules so you can start connecting faster.
Are kids allowed?
We are a community-focused space! Kids are welcome, though we ask that children under 13 be accompanied by an adult. We have a great selection of family-friendly titles.
Do you serve food and drinks?
We try to partner with our hosting locations to offer their delicious food and drinks to purchase. If requested, we are happy to provide snack and drink options as well.

Still have questions?

We're here to help! Whether you're planning a party or just curious about a specific game, feel free to reach out.
