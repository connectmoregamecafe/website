Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Board Game Cafe?

A board game cafe is a social space where you can gather with friends to enjoy a curated library of tabletop games while grabbing a drink or a snack. It’s a place designed to help people disconnect from screens and Connect More with each other.

How does the "Library Fee" work?

For a small flat fee, you get unlimited access to our curated collection of board games for the entire pop-up.

Can I bring my own games?

Absolutely! You are welcome to bring your own games. Standard table rates still apply to cover the space.

Do you teach people how to play?

Yes! Our "Game Connectors" are always on hand to help you pick a game, set it up, and explain the rules.
