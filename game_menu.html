Animal Upon Animal

▼
The Goal: Be the first to place all your wooden animals on the stack.
How to Play: Roll the die to see if you place animals or let the group decide where they go.
Connector Tip: Steady hands! If the stack falls, you have to take more animals back.
Gateway • 2-4 Players • Easy

Azul

▼
The Goal: Draft tiles to create beautiful patterns on your palace wall.
How to Play: Pick all tiles of one color from a factory. Place them in rows; full rows move to your wall.
Connector Tip: Don't take tiles you can't fit! They drop to the floor and cost you points.
Gateway • 3-4 Players • Intermediate

Catan

▼
The Goal: Build settlements and roads to reach 10 Victory Points.
How to Play: Roll dice for resources. Use Wheat, Sheep, Wood, Brick, and Ore to expand.
Connector Tip: Trading with other players is key. Don't be afraid to make an offer!
Table Talk • 2-8 Players • Easy

Codenames

▼
The Goal: Contact all agents based on one-word clues from your Spymaster.
How to Play: Teams take turns guessing cards. Avoid the "Assassin" card at all costs!
Connector Tip: Look for clues that link multiple cards together to win faster.
2-Player • 2 Players • Easy

Jaipur

▼
The Goal: Be the best merchant by collecting and selling goods for gold.
How to Play: Take cards from the market or sell sets of goods. Use camels to swap multiple cards.
Connector Tip: Big sales (3+ cards) give you bonus point tokens!
Classic • 2-4 Players • Intermediate

Scrabble

▼
The Goal: Build high-scoring words that connect to existing tiles on the board.
How to Play: Play a word, calculate points based on letter values and board bonuses.
Connector Tip: Two-letter words are your best friend for tight spots.
Table Talk • 2-5 Players • Easy

Sushi Go!

▼
The Goal: Pick the best sushi cards to score the most points over 3 rounds.
How to Play: Pick one card to keep, then pass your hand to the player next to you.
Connector Tip: Collecting "Maki" rolls is a race—the player with the most gets the bonus!
Strategy • 1-5 Players • Advanced

Wingspan

▼
The Goal: Discover and attract the best birds to your wildlife habitat.
How to Play: Spend food to play birds. Birds give you powers that trigger when you gather food or lay eggs.
Connector Tip: Pay attention to your "End of Round" goals for extra points.
