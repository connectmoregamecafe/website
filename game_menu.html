Gateway Game

Azul

▼
The Goal Score points by drafting tiles to decorate the palace wall. Don't drop any!
How to Play Pick all tiles of one color from a display. Place them on your staging rows. Once a row is full, one tile moves to your wall.
Guru Tip Watch your floor line! Excess tiles count against your score at the end of the round.
Classic

Ticket to Ride

▼
The Goal Connect North American cities with your train cars to fulfill destination tickets.
How to Play On your turn, either draw colored train cards, claim a route by spending cards, or draw new destination tickets.
Guru Tip Long routes score big points, but don't ignore your tickets—incomplete ones lose you points!
