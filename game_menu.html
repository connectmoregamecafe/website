Quick Start Guides

The Classics

Scrabble

▼
The Goal Score the most points by creating interlocking words on the board using letter tiles.
How to Play On your turn, use tiles from your rack to form a word. Words must touch at least one tile already on the board. Draw back up to 7 tiles.
Connector Tip Aim for the "Premium Squares" (Double/Triple Word) to skyrocket your score!
Gateway Game

Azul

▼
The Goal Draft colored tiles to complete patterns on your player board.
How to Play Pick all tiles of one color from a factory circle. Place them on your staging rows. At the end of the round, "wall" your tiles to score.
Connector Tip If you take tiles you can't use, they fall to the floor and cost you negative points!
Gateway Game

Catan

▼
The Goal Be the first to reach 10 Victory Points by building settlements, cities, and roads.
How to Play Roll dice to collect resources based on where your houses are. Trade resources with other players to get what you need to build.
Connector Tip Don't be afraid to haggle! Trading is the heart of the game.
Two-Player Duel

Jaipur

▼
The Goal Be the most successful merchant in the Maharaja’s court by trading goods for tokens.
How to Play On your turn, either take cards (goods or camels) from the market or sell goods for points.
Connector Tip The earlier you sell a specific type of good, the more points it's worth!
Table Talk

Codenames

▼
The Goal Find all your team's secret agents before the other team does.
How to Play The "Spymaster" gives a one-word clue and a number (e.g., "Animal: 3"). The team tries to pick the correct cards on the table.
Connector Tip Avoid the Assassin card! If you pick it, your team loses instantly.
Table Talk

Sushi Go!

▼
The Goal Create the best "meal" of sushi combinations.
How to Play Pick one card from your hand, place it face down, and pass the rest to the player on your left. Everyone reveals at once.
Connector Tip Pudding cards don't score until the very end of the game—don't ignore them!
Strategy

Wingspan

▼
The Goal Attract the best variety of birds to your wildlife preserves.
How to Play Play bird cards to your board, which then makes your "Lay Eggs," "Gain Food," and "Draw Cards" actions more powerful.
Connector Tip Look for "Engine Building" birds—cards that give you bonuses every time you take an action in that row.
Youth Table

Animal Upon Animal

▼
The Goal Be the first to place all your wooden animals on the stack.
How to Play Roll the die to see if you place one animal, two animals, or let the group decide.
Connector Tip If the stack falls, you have to take up to two of the fallen animals into your pile!
